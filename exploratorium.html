John Muir Trail, Day 1

September 2, 2019

Role + Team

In this brief internship, my work was mostly independent with opportunities for feedback from a few Exploratorium exhibit developers. There was a broader team that I met with a few times and ultimately presented to.

Challenge

The Exploratorium is planning a new museum in Brazil. One of the three themes to be present in the museum is "Imagining the Future." This is very broad, so we needed to determine how it would manifest in its context.

Outcome

Using a systems approach and design thinking, I gave exhibit developers explicit goals and steps to work toward a museum that gives every Brazilian has confidence in their ability to change the future.
