About  Me

Currently a junior at Stanford University, I am studying to become a UX Designer. User focused design brings together my many passions: understanding people, creating new technologies, and facilitating teamwork. I am working towards making a difference in mental healthcare and believe user-focused technology will provide a much needed solution. In my spare time, I enjoys outdoor adventures and cheap travel; feel free to ask me to share stories!
